I am Chawlie

Charlie Chauvin

I believe that every person has a great story to tell.

Most people just don’t know how to get their story across where it makes sense to others. That’s where I come in. I’m a designer, a coder, a drawer, a painter and someone who is just always busy creating. What drives me is solving puzzles and helping individuals understand the essence of what they need to say so that their story is clear to others.

Recently I have been working with clients and team members to build content strategy, wire frame user flows, create hierarchy for design and devising ways to carry the vision through development. I sweat the small stuff and build logical user experiences with everything that I create.

I love to collaborate and I'm always looking for a new creative adventure. I am a person who loves to be constantly learning so staying active outside work has always been important for me. You will often find me building websites, painting signs or drawing to help flex my mind muscles.

I have a background in print design, but a real passion for the web. There are many moving parts and I love to create large working systems. Every website has a unique problem from beginning to end and requires a strategic solution in order to deliver a great experience.
